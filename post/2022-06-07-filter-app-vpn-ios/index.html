Published

2022-06-07

Trouble with VPN (Loon)

Though the filter rule support local and self-defined resource parser, GeoIP resolver, etc. I still came across many times where I had trouble with Loon enabled.

This is one of easiest case involved, I would like certain app to follow certain policy, such as REJECT, DIRECT or proxy (added).

It can be achieved as followed, where all the traffic via Facebook app will be blocked.

[Rule]
USER-AGENT,com.facebook.Facebook,REJECT

Examples can be found here: https://github.com/blackmatrix7/ios_rule_script/blob/master/rule/Loon/Apple/Apple.list
